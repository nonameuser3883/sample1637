# UX Copy — MyDrama App

UI copywriting rules. Hard specs for screen dimensions and language live in [INSTRUCTIONS.md](INSTRUCTIONS.md).

## UX writing principles

- **Concise** — shortest phrasing that stays clear. Cut filler words ("please", "kindly", "the").
- **Action-oriented CTAs** — verbs, present tense. "Unlock", "Watch", "Continue", not "Unlocking" or "To unlock".
- **Scannable** — key info first, details after. Users skim, they don't read.
- **Friendly, not corporate** — "Let's go" over "Proceed". "Oops, try again" over "An error occurred".
- **No jargon** — "Try again" not "Retry operation". "Connect" not "Establish connection".
- **Error messages** — what happened + what to do next. Never scary or technical.
- **Empty states** — explain what goes here + how to fill it. Use it as onboarding.
- **Button labels** — under 3 words when possible. 1–2 words is ideal.
- **Sentence case** for buttons, labels, titles (not Title Case, not UPPERCASE).
- **No trailing punctuation** on buttons, labels, or short headings.
- **Numbers over words** for quantities (`3 episodes` not `three episodes`), except at start of sentence.
- **Use active voice.** "You unlocked this episode" not "This episode has been unlocked by you".
- **Be specific over generic.** "Watch episode 5" over "Continue watching".

## Tone

- Confident but warm — a smart friend, not a salesperson
- Playful when the moment is right (empty states, celebrations), restrained otherwise
- No exclamation marks unless genuinely exciting. Max 1 per screen.
- No emojis in core UI copy unless they serve a functional purpose

## Examples

### Good

- `Watch without limits`
- `Unlock now`
- `This episode is paid — tap to unlock`
- `You're offline. Check your connection.`
- `No episodes yet. Subscribe to get the first one.`
- `Skip`
- `Continue`

### Bad

- `Click here to proceed to the next step` (too long, "click here" is dead)
- `Нажмите чтобы продолжить` (wrong language)
- `ERROR 500: SERVER UNAVAILABLE` (technical, scary)
- `Please kindly tap the unlock button` (filler words)
- `Unlocking the episode now...` (verbose, use present verb on button)
- `Watch Now!` (Title Case + unnecessary exclamation)
